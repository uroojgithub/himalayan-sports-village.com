  • Present your ID card and register according to the requirements of the club.
  • In most cases, a single reciprocal club is restricted for a maximum of 15 visits in a year and a maximum of 7 days in a month.
  • Cash payments must be made for all kinds of facilities used at the club.
  • Booking can be made directly or through the club in case sufficient time is available, since some clubs demand advance for confirmation of bookings as their requirement. A members has to pay by demand draft directly under intimation to us along with details of booking etc.
